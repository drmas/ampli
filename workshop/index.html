What We Need From You

Speaker Preparation

Please prepare the following materials before the workshop date, including requirements for attendees.

Profile Information

Full Name As you'd like it displayed on event materials
Professional Headshot High resolution, minimum 400x400px, professional quality
Job Title & Company Current role and organization
Short Bio 50-100 words about your background and expertise
LinkedIn Profile URL For attendees who want to connect
Twitter/X Handle Optional — for social media promotion

Workshop Content

Workshop Title Clear, engaging title that describes the session
Workshop Description 150-200 words explaining what attendees will learn and build
Key Learning Outcomes 3-5 bullet points of what attendees will take away
Target Audience Who is this workshop best suited for? Skill level, background
Attendee Technical Requirements Laptop specs, software to install, accounts to create, files to download, prior knowledge expected

We'll promote the event using your provided materials. You're welcome to share on your channels too!

Code of Conduct

We maintain a welcoming, inclusive environment. All participants agree to respectful, professional conduct.
